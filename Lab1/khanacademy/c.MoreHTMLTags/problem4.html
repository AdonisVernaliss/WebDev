Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 10 min
Ingredients Quantity
Miso paste 1,5-2 tbsp.
Soy curd "Tofu" 100-150 g
Wakame Algae 1-2 tbsp.
Soy sauce to taste
Green onions to taste
Ginger 2 cm.
Onion 1 pc.
Carrot 1 pc.
Garlick 3-4 clove

Step 1: First we need to wash the ginger, and also peel the onion, garlic and carrots. We send all the components into a saucepan and fill them with water. Cut the ginger into small pieces, leave the garlic whole, and cut the carrots and onions into four parts. Here we also add allspice (peas).

Step2:Cook the broth until the vegetables are soft. Then filter through a sieve.

Step3:Now we pour dried Wakame algae into the liquid. Mix everything thoroughly and cook over low heat. The mass should not boil.

Step4:After that, using a small sieve, we dissolve the miso paste in the broth.

Step5:Tofu cheese is cut into neat cubes of medium size. We send them to the broth and mix everything. If desired, add hot sauce.

Step6:After that, the soup is ready. We send it to serving plates. Sprinkle chopped green onions on top.After that, the soup is ready. We send it to serving plates. Sprinkle chopped green onions on top.

Step7:Before serving, miso soup is decorated with sesame seeds. Enjoy your meal!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 2 min
  • Serves: 1 min
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 piece
Sousages or meat to taste
Cheese to tase
Tomato 2 slice
Salad 1 lettuce leaf

Step 1: Lay out the two slices of bread on a clean surface.

Step2:Spread mayonnaise or mustard on one or both slices of bread (optional).

Step3:Place the deli meat on one slice of bread.

Step4:Add the lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and onion on top of the deli meat.

Step5:Place the second slice of bread on top of the vegetables to make a sandwich.

Step6:Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!

Recipe #3

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 10 min
Ingredients Quantity
cooked white rice 1 cup
vegetable oil 2 tbsp.
diced onion 1/4 cup
diced carrots 1/4 cup
frozen peas 1/4 cup
beaten eggs 2
soy sauce 2 tbsp.
Salt and pepper to taste

Step 1: Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat.

Step2:Add vegetable oil, onion, carrots and peas. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until vegetables are tender.

Step3:Push the vegetables to the side of the skillet and add beaten eggs to the other side. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked.

Step4:Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper and stir to combine. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the rice is heated through.

Step5:Serve and enjoy!
